CPI & PPI News

Forex traders closely monitor Consumer Price Index (CPI) and Producer Price Index (PPI) for economic health insights. CPI gauges inflation in consumer goods, while PPI measures inflation in production. Fluctuations impact currency values, with even a 0.1% change garnering attention. Stay informed for strategic trading


Forex traders closely monitor Consumer Price Index (CPI) and Producer Price Index (PPI) as key economic indicators. A higher-than-expected CPI may lead to currency appreciation, while elevated PPI could signal inflationary pressure, impacting currency values by several percentage points.
